プライバシーポリシー Privacy Policy
tl;dr — We collect nothing. We know nothing. The app runs entirely on your device. The AI coach is powered by Apple Intelligence and never connects to any server.

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Anki N1-JLPT Test does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data whatsoever. We have no servers, no analytics pipeline, no backend. There is no account to create and no login required.

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics, crash reports sent to us, or telemetry
  • No location data
  • No purchase history beyond what Apple manages directly

2. Your Study Data

All study progress, test scores, flashcard performance, streak data, and AI-generated study plans are stored exclusively in your device's local storage (Core Data / iCloud sync if you have enabled iCloud). This data is yours — we never access it, and it never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up yourself via iCloud.

3. AI Study Coach — Apple Intelligence

The AI Study Coach feature is powered by Apple's on-device language model (requires iOS 26+ and Apple Intelligence enabled). All AI inference happens locally on your processor. No prompts, responses, performance data, or any other content is sent to any server. Apple's own privacy commitments for Apple Intelligence apply — see apple.com/privacy.

4. Audio

Native Japanese audio is bundled within the app. No network request is made to stream or fetch audio content. The microphone is never accessed.

5. Subscriptions & Purchases

Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple's App Store via StoreKit. We never see your payment card details, Apple ID, or transaction information.

6. Third-Party SDKs

The app contains no third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Facebook SDK, no Crashlytics). Apple's default crash reporting (opt-in by users in iOS settings) may collect anonymised diagnostics governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

7. Children

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone. Because no data is collected from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Privacy questions? Email us at important041994@gmail.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AI coach really on-device?
Yes. It uses Apple's on-device language model introduced in iOS 26 with Apple Intelligence. No data is sent to any server.

Which iOS version do I need?
iOS 26 or later is required for the AI Study Coach. All other features work on iOS 16+.

Can I switch the study plan language?
Yes — your AI-generated plan can be translated into any of the 13 supported languages with a single tap.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone and select Anki N1-JLPT Test.

Where is my study data stored?
Entirely on your device. If you have iCloud Drive enabled, iOS may back up the data to your personal iCloud account — never accessible by us.
