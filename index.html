Privacy Policy

Our Privacy-First Philosophy

We believe your study data belongs to you. Anki N1, JLPT Test AI powered is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No personal data collection
  • No third-party tracking
  • No advertisements
  • Full offline functionality

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Study progress or quiz results to external servers
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Location data
  • User behavior analytics
  • Contacts or photos

Information Processed Locally

The following data is processed exclusively on your device and never leaves it:

Study Data

  • Quiz and test results
  • Study progress and streak information
  • Custom vocabulary cards you create
  • Performance analytics for AI Study Coach
  • Anki export history

App Preferences

  • Interface settings and customization
  • Language preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Subscription status

🔒 Apple Intelligence Integration

When you use AI Coach features on compatible devices (iOS 18.1+):

  • Processing Location: AI analysis happens entirely on-device
  • No Cloud Upload: Your study data is never sent to external servers
  • Apple's Privacy: Processing follows Apple's strict privacy standards
  • Your Control: Disable AI features anytime in Settings

Subscription and Payment

Subscription purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store:

  • Payment Processing: Handled exclusively by Apple
  • What We Receive: Only subscription status (active/expired)
  • What We Don't See: Credit cards, billing addresses, payment history

Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored in your device's encrypted app sandbox, protected by iOS security features including device passcode/biometric authentication.

⚠️ Important: No Remote Servers

We do not operate any remote servers that store user data. No user accounts, no cloud synchronization. Your data stays on your device.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Immediate Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data
  • Reset Option: Reset all progress in Settings
  • No Remote Copies: We have no remote copies to delete

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is Anki N1, JLPT Test AI powered?

Anki N1 is a comprehensive study app designed specifically for mastering the JLPT N1 level. It combines AI-powered personalized study plans, full-length practice tests, intelligent flashcards, and Anki export functionality.

❓ Do I need an internet connection?

No! The app works 100% offline after initial download. All study materials, AI coaching, and practice tests are available without internet.

❓ What's included in Pro subscription?

  • All 30 full-length practice tests
  • AI Study Coach with personalized plans
  • 749+ premium vocabulary flashcards
  • Unlimited Anki exports
  • Streak Freeze protection
  • Real-time JLPT Pass Prediction
  • Custom card editor
  • Complete offline access

❓ How much does Pro cost?

  • Monthly: $5.99/month
  • Annual: $34.99/year (Save ~50%!)

❓ How do I cancel my subscription?

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Anki N1, JLPT Test AI powered
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

❓ How does the AI Study Coach work?

The AI Coach analyzes your quiz and test performance to identify weak areas, then generates a precise 30-day study plan. It uses on-device Apple Intelligence processing, so your data never leaves your device.

❓ How do I export to Anki?

  1. Complete a practice test
  2. Review your answers
  3. Tap the Anki Export button
  4. Choose where to save the .apkg file
  5. Import into Anki app or Anki desktop

Technical Issues

🔧 App crashes when opening

  1. Force quit the app (swipe up from app switcher)
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check for app updates in App Store
  4. If persists, contact support with device model and iOS version

🔧 Audio doesn't play

  • Check device isn't on silent mode
  • Ensure volume is turned up
  • Try disconnecting/reconnecting Bluetooth headphones
  • Restart the app

🔧 Subscription not recognized

  1. Open the App
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Restore Purchases
  4. Wait for process to complete
